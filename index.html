A little about me

My name is Branden Langhals and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer. As far back as I can remember I have enjoyed taking things apart to see how they work. My web-journey began in 2000, using simple HTML to customize my MySpace page. Time-travel 20 years later and I now know how things work in a more professional way. I am currently working on formal education studying Full-Stack Web Development at Case Western Reserve University in sunny Cleveland, Ohio.


I am a software developer with lots of humility. Experienced in MVC, HTML, CSS, javaScript, NodeJS, jQuery, SQL, MongoDB, noSQL, bootstrap, Express, JSON, Ajax, Git, React, sequelize, and Visual Studio. Plese take a look at my projects section to see some of the work I have done. Or, if you have any questions, please reach out to me at BrandenLanghals@gmail.com
