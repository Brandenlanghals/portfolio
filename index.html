A little about me

My name is Branden Langhals and I am a Full-Stack Web Developer. As far back as I can remember I have enjoyed taking things apart to see how they work. When I was 7 I threw marbles at my parent's tv screen because I wanted to see what was inside a marble, and also what was inside our families TV. My web-journey began in 2000, using simple HTML to customize my MySpace page. Time-travel 20 years later and I have started to learn how things work in a more resposible way. I am currently working on a formal education studying Full-Stack Web Development at Case Western Reserve University.


I will continue to update this portfolio as my journey continues, and new codes are added to my fingers. Also, if you know whats inside a marble, or would like more stories about the weird things I did when I was a kid, use the contact link above.
